NUST is a multi-campus university spread all over Pakistan. Its central campus is in Islamabad with other campuses at Rawalpindi, Risalpur and Karachi. The CENTRAL CAMPUS has been established at Sector H-12, Islamabad to cater for the fast-growing requirements of a technologically advanced campus of science and technology. Besides the Rectorate, new high-tech postgraduate schools, centers and institutes have been established at this campus. Undergraduate and postgraduate programs have commenced from Fall 2008 and a number of new programs have commenced since Fall 2013.

There are three campuses in Rawalpindi:

  • College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (CEME)
  • Military College of Signals (MCS)
  • NUST Institute of Peace and Conflict Studies (NIPCONS)

    • There are two campuses in Risalpur:

  • College of Aeronautical Engineering (CAE)
  • Military College of Engineering (MCE)

    • Karachi Campus:

  • Pakistan Naval Engineering College (PNEC)
